Echoes of Time: A Poetry Collection

Whispers of the Wind

The wind whispers secrets at night,
Soft voices dancing in pale moonlight.
A story unfolds on the ocean's crest,
A lullaby sung as the world finds rest.

Silent Footsteps

Footsteps echo in the hollow streets,
Ghostly whispers where silence meets.
A tale untold in the midnight air,
Shadows lingering, lost in despair.
